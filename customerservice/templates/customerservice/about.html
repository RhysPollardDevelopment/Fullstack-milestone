About Us

Who We Are

We started Freebees to find a way to help the bees in our country; the honey bees we think of most often, and the many other species of wild bee that are being harmed and spoken of much less.

So we decided to partner up with like minded groups to try and tackle both at once! Free bees and you can get some tasty freebies (witty no).

Sting In the Tale

The current form of commercial beekeeping forces the production of honey from hives forced to grow queens, existing on harsh sugars while their honey is harvested beyond reasonable levels.

Bees are smoked, treated with harsh chemicals and all at the cost of the welfare and life span of the bees themselves.

Add on the delivery of honey from other countries and all the ethical concerns...

It can be alot to think about, but we aren't here to cast blame.

But Things Can Bee Better

The point is it can be better, and that's why we are here!

Your subscription helps fund the beekeepers who really care about their swarms, and with our funding we can donate to the charities which will help preserve our bees and wildlife buzzing along.

What Makes Us So Different

Our partners do the closest form of beekeeping as possible to just leaving the bees alone, sometimes called natural or biodynamic beekeeping.

No unneccesary pesticides, antibiotics, no forced queens or overwinter killing. Just pollen, bees and whatever remains after winter.
