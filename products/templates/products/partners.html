Our Partners

We are honored to be partnered with a group of amazing apiaists and farms on an ongoing basis, all working towards a more considerate, natural view on beekeeping and producing honey. Through donations, expertise and the unbeliable work of our community we can give back to and support our country's wildlife.

So a big thanks to our partners for doing all they do to keep their bees happy, countryside wild and beautiful and for promoting all the values we hold in the name of keeping honey from becoming bittersweet.

Become part of the hive

Already speaking our language?

Do you already farm honey naturally? Are you a interested in expanding your scope to a larger audience? Then contact us regarding joining our team as a partner! We'd love to welcome more members into our bee alliance and help make things just that bit better.

Looking to change stripes?

If you're interested in learning how to change your honey collecting methods to a natural, bee friendly system then we'd love to have you onboard! Contact us and we can discuss the changes you'd need to make and help you achieve them.

If you don't want to commit to a full change, but want advice on how to improve your bees' welfare then we still would love to hear from you!
